Gravity Falls

Gravity Falls is an American animated mystery-comedy series created by Alex Hirsch. It premiered on June 15, 2012, and the story follows 12-year-old twins Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend the summer with their great-uncle Stan at the Mystery Shack in the small town of Gravity Falls, Oregon. Across two seasons (40 episodes), the show blends supernatural mystery, running storylines, and puzzle-driven Easter eggs — including the famous "Journal 3."

MYSTERY & SERIALIZATION

Gravity Falls blends episodic comedy with a long-running mystery involving hidden ciphers, secret journals, and an overarching antagonist.

CHARACTER-DRIVEN HUMOR

The show is known for its sharp, character-based comedy and heartfelt moments that develop the twins, their friends, and the town's residents.

Creators & Characters

Created by Alex Hirsch, Gravity Falls mixes clever mysteries and heartfelt character arcs. Key characters include Dipper and Mabel Pines, Grunkle Stan, Soos, and Wendy.

MEET THE CAST

Main Characters

Dipper Pines

The curious, analytical twin who discovers Journal 3 and leads many of the show's investigations.

Mabel Pines

Dipper's energetic and optimistic twin whose creativity and empathy balance the show's mystery elements.

Grunkle Stan

The scheming proprietor of the Mystery Shack. He serves as a guardian figure with hidden depths and secrets of his own.

Soos Ramirez

A lovable, well-meaning handyman at the Mystery Shack and a loyal friend to the Pines family.

Wendy Corduroy

A cool, resourceful teenager who works at the Mystery Shack and befriends the Pines twins.

Bill Cipher

An interdimensional dream demon and the series' major antagonist, known for chaos and mind-bending schemes.

Series Impact

Gravity Falls has influenced modern animated storytelling with its serialized mystery structure, cross-age appeal, and dedicated fan-driven puzzle-solving community.
