Gravity Falls: Mystery in the Pines

Gravity Falls is an American mystery-comedy animated series created by Alex Hirsch. It follows twins Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend the summer with their great-uncle Stan in the mysterious town of Gravity Falls, Oregon.

Characters & Cast

Dipper Pines

Curious twelve-year-old twin who finds Journal 3 and investigates Gravity Falls' mysteries.

Mabel Pines

Energetic, optimistic twin known for sweaters, quirky hobbies, and fierce loyalty to family.

Grunkle Stan

Mysterious proprietor of the Mystery Shack; Dipper and Mabel's great-uncle with hidden secrets.

Soos

Good-natured handyman at the Mystery Shack who helps the twins in many investigations.

Wendy Corduroy

Laid-back teenage employee at the Mystery Shack and friend of Dipper and Mabel.

Bill Cipher

Interdimensional dream demon and primary antagonist who manipulates reality and minds.

Series Facts

Gravity Falls originally aired from 2012 to 2016 across two seasons and 40 episodes. It was created by Alex Hirsch and is known for its interconnected mysteries and hidden codes.
